Mad Science Presents:

A Sobering Experiment

October 1 - 28, 2015

Alcohol and boozing has been around for centuries. It's a cultural norm to have alcoholic drinks at social gatherings. It's ingrained in our nature, we see advertisements on tv, billboards, magazines, social media.

We talk about how we got wasted on the weekend and laugh about our alcohol fueled antics on our coffee breaks. At the end of a hard day of work, we go home and have a beer.

Does your life suck? Why not drown your sorrows with a drink

Did you get a promotion?! Let's have a celebratory drink.

A Sobering Experiment is my dive into life without alcohol.

Hypothesis:

Can I stay sober for 4 weeks? What will be the effects of having 0 alcohol in my body for 4 weeks? Will my mood, energy and general outlook on life change at all? What triggers will I experience and how will I overcome them?

Procedure:

I will not consume any alcohol for 4 weeks, during that time I will take recordings on a daily basis, items will be measured on a scale from 1 - 5 at the end of each week will I will review my daily recordings and form a weekly summary. At the end of 4 weeks I will prepare a formal review of all the facts gathered during the 4 week experiment.

Daily Questions:

  • What time did I wake up?
    • 1 - 5 How did I feel when I woke up?
  • When did I go to bed?
    • 1 - 5 How long it took to fall asleep?
  • 1 - 5 What was my energy level throughout the day?
  • 1 - 5 What was my mood today?
  • 1 - 5 What was my outlook on life?
  • 1 - 5 Did I want an alcoholic drink today?

Weekly Questions:

  • How much do I weight?
  • Did I experience any times I wanted a drink this week?
    • What was the situation, who was there, where they drinking?
    • What did I do?

Prediction:

I predict I will be able to stay sober for an entire 4 weeks, my hardest challenges will be during my 4 day kayaking trip, at family events and when hanging out with friends. I feel I will save a noticeable amount of money, lose 2 pounds, wake up feeling more refreshed and have more energy throughout the day.
