Wet Dreams Surf Camp

Problem:

You want to surf around Jordan River on your three days off, but you don't want to drive back and forth everyday or camp in the cold and the rain.

Lets face it! Driving 140km three days in a row is bad for the environment and it's not cheap!

Solution:

Wet Dreams Surf Camp

Situated in the quite hamlet of Jordan River, the Wet Dreams Surf Camp is a solid roof and a dry bed for anybody looking to surf and spend some quality time on the southern west coast of Vancouver Island

Benefits:

Stay @ The Surf

Spend less time driving and more time surfing!

Hot Showers!

You know you get out of the water nearly hypothermic, now you can jump in a hot shower right after your surf

Jordan River Is Off The Grid

Get away from it all, including cell reception

When you staying at the Wet Dreams Surf Camp:

  • You'll surf more!
    • Which means you'll have more fun and get better
  • Meet like minded people.
  • You'll get a chance to unwind and get grounded on the west coast.
